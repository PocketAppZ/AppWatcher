App Watcher for Android

App Watcher notifies you about updates for application from Play Store even the app is not installed currently on your device and provides quick access to the "What's new" section

Features:

  • Search and add app from Play Store to watch list
  • Manual and automatic updates
  • Quick shortcut to see what's new in app
  • Share application
  • Add app from Play Store
  • Backup of the list
